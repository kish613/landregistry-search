# Licensing

The underlying data is **public** and released under permissive terms.
The index, software, and brand are our own; the licences below cover
the bits you'll typically need to attribute.

## Source data — Open Government Licence v3.0

CCOD and OCOD are published by HM Land Registry under the
**[Open Government Licence v3.0](https://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/doc/open-government-licence/version/3/)**.

You are free to:

- Copy, publish, distribute, and transmit the data.
- Adapt and combine it with other data.
- Use it commercially.

Provided you:

- **Attribute** the source.
- Don't suggest official endorsement.
- Don't misuse personal data (mostly relevant for OCOD addresses).

### Suggested attribution

For data shown on a web page or in a report:

> Source: HM Land Registry Commercial and Corporate Ownership Data
> (CCOD), released under the Open Government Licence v3.0, indexed
> via landregistry.company.

For data in a spreadsheet shared with colleagues:

> Contains HM Land Registry data © Crown copyright and database right
> {year}. This data is licensed under the Open Government Licence v3.0.

If you use a director-search export, also credit Companies House:

> Contains Companies House data © Crown copyright. Reproduced under
> the Open Government Licence v3.0.

## Companies House data

Officer search results (used in director search) come from the
[Companies House public API](https://developer.company-information.service.gov.uk/),
also under Open Government Licence v3.0. Same attribution practice
applies.

## Press and data requests

For press use, bulk data, or licensing questions:
