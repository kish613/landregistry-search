FAQ

Common questions about corporate ownership search

Short answers for legal and due-diligence teams using company ownership search as a screening workflow.

How often is the data refreshed?

Monthly dataset refresh. The site’s messaging is aligned to the HM Land Registry CCOD release cadence rather than making a misleading daily-update claim.

Is this a free consumer property search?

No. The site is positioned as a paid-search workflow for legal and due-diligence use cases, with clear paths into the live registry search and supporting guides.

Where should I start?

Start with Company Property Search UK if you know the workflow you need, or use the live search tool if you already have the entity or site information ready.
