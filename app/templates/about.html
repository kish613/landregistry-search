About the platform

Built for legal and due-diligence workflows

LandRegistry.company is positioned as a fast screening layer for corporate ownership questions. It helps legal, property, and research teams move from entity questions to likely titles and sites faster.

Source data

HM Land Registry CCOD

The platform is built around HM Land Registry Commercial and Corporate Ownership Data for England and Wales and framed honestly as a monthly-refresh dataset.

Workflow

Entity first, title second

The core use case is screening a company, director, or address before deciding which title documents or specialist searches deserve the next stage of work.

Data methodology
