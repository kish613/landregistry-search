Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 4, 2026

Summary

LandRegistry.company provides free access to corporate land ownership data. We keep data collection minimal and use cookies for analytics and advertising. You can choose whether ads are personalized in the consent banner.

Information We Collect

  • Account details you provide (email address, hashed password if you set one).
  • Basic usage analytics (page views and feature usage) to improve the product.
  • Advertising data collected by Google (see below) to serve and measure ads.

Advertising & Cookies

We use Google AdSense to support the service. Google may use cookies or similar technologies to serve ads, measure performance, and, if you allow, personalize ads. You can opt for non-personalized ads at any time using the consent banner on the site.

If you are in the UK/EU, we default to non-personalized ads until you choose otherwise.

Data Sources

We use official public data from HM Land Registry (CCOD dataset) and Companies House. We do not sell this data or your account information to third parties.

Contact

For privacy questions, email noreply@landregistry.company.
