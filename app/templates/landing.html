Search methods

Three ways to interrogate the register.

Each method queries the same index — use whichever matches what you know.

Company search

Locate every title registered to a corporate proprietor. Matches on exact name, fuzzy name, or 8-digit Companies House number.

  • Fuzzy name match
  • CRN lookup
  • Group / subsidiary trace
£1.00 per search Try it

Address lookup

Reverse lookup. Enter a postcode or site address to identify the registered corporate proprietor — freehold or leasehold.

  • Postcode (full or partial)
  • USRN / UPRN
  • Street-level match
£1.00 per search Try it

Director search

Cross-references director appointment history with CCOD — surfaces titles held by companies where a named person is (or was) a director.

  • Resigned appointments
  • Associated entities
  • Date-bounded search
£3.00 · premium Try it
Data source

The register, indexed and searchable.

The dataset covers every freehold and leasehold interest registered to a corporate proprietor in England and Wales. We mirror HM Land Registry's Commercial and Corporate Ownership Data (CCOD), refreshed monthly, and layer a fast search index plus Companies House cross-reference on top.
