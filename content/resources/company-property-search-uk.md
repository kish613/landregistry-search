<h2>Search Official HM Land Registry CCOD Data</h2>
<p>For legal and due-diligence teams, the goal is not a generic property lookup. The goal is to identify whether a company appears in the <strong>Commercial and Corporate Ownership Data</strong> published by HM Land Registry, then cross-check the owner details against the company record you are investigating.</p>

<h3>When this workflow is useful</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Pre-acquisition diligence on a target company or SPV</li>
  <li>Ownership tracing for a disputed site, redevelopment, or notice process</li>
  <li>Initial screening before ordering title documents or running specialist searches</li>
</ul>

<h3>Recommended search sequence</h3>
<ol>
  <li>Search by company number where possible, because it is the cleanest way to separate similarly named entities.</li>
  <li>Search by company name to catch naming variations, abbreviations, and filings that differ from the marketing name.</li>
  <li>Use address search if you already know the site and need to identify the relevant corporate owner quickly.</li>
</ol>

<h3>What to verify after a match</h3>
<p>Once a company match is returned, compare the proprietor name, title number, and correspondence data against Companies House details. If the matter is material, treat the CCOD match as a <strong>screening and workflow accelerator</strong>, then escalate to the official title documents you need for the transaction or investigation.</p>
