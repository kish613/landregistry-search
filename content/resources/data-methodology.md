<h2>Source data and refresh cycle</h2>
<p>LandRegistry.company is built on <a href="https://use-land-property-data.service.gov.uk/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">HM Land Registry Commercial and Corporate Ownership Data</a>. The platform is positioned as a fast screening interface for legal and due-diligence teams, not as a substitute for the formal title material required in a transaction.</p>

<h3>Methodology principles</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Prefer exact entity identifiers where they exist.</li>
  <li>Use search-friendly normalization to make company-name and address matching more practical.</li>
  <li>Treat dataset refresh cadence honestly: the source is released on a monthly schedule, so the site uses <strong>monthly dataset refresh</strong> messaging throughout.</li>
</ul>
