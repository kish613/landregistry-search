<h2>These records answer different questions</h2>
<p><a href="https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Companies House</a> tells you about the entity: filings, officers, registered office, and corporate history. <a href="https://www.gov.uk/search-property-information-land-registry" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">HM Land Registry</a> tells you about the land or title. For due diligence, neither source replaces the other.</p>

<h3>Use them together</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Use Companies House to confirm the exact entity and its number.</li>
  <li>Use CCOD search to screen whether that entity appears as a corporate owner.</li>
  <li>Escalate to title material where you need formal certainty on the legal estate.</li>
</ul>
