<h2>Use CCOD to screen commercial ownership questions fast</h2>
<p>Commercial property due diligence often starts with a practical question: <strong>which company owns this site, and how broadly does that ownership extend?</strong> HM Land Registry’s CCOD dataset is a strong first-pass answer for England and Wales because it surfaces corporate and commercial ownership records at scale.</p>

<h3>Best-fit use cases</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Development and compulsory purchase notice preparation</li>
  <li>Land assembly and neighbouring title investigations</li>
  <li>Portfolio reviews for lenders, investors, and restructuring teams</li>
</ul>

<h3>How to use the result set</h3>
<p>Look for title concentration, repeat proprietor names, and correspondence patterns. If a commercial site sits inside a wider network of titles or SPVs, the value is often in the pattern rather than the first single match. Pair the CCOD view with Companies House and the title documents required for legal certainty.</p>
