<h2>Use CCOD for screening, title documents for formal reliance</h2>
<p>CCOD is excellent for early-stage diligence because it lets you find corporate ownership patterns fast. A title register is the document you reach for when you need the official record, restrictive covenant detail, mortgage information, or a document suitable for formal legal work.</p>

<h3>A simple rule of thumb</h3>
<ul>
  <li><strong>CCOD:</strong> good for portfolio screening, entity tracing, and target identification.</li>
  <li><strong>Title register:</strong> good for transaction-critical verification and matters where the formal record is required.</li>
</ul>

<p>For many teams, the best process is to screen with CCOD first, narrow the scope, then order title material only where it is necessary.</p>
