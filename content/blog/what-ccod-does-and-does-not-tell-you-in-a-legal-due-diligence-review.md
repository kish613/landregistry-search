<h2>CCOD answers the screening question, not every legal question</h2>
<p>CCOD is strong when you need to know whether a company appears in the corporate ownership dataset and what titles or property references are likely relevant. It is not the final word on every encumbrance, covenant, or transaction issue.</p>

<h3>Use CCOD to:</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Screen a company or site quickly</li>
  <li>Find likely corporate ownership matches</li>
  <li>Prioritise what requires deeper review</li>
</ul>

<h3>Do not rely on CCOD alone to:</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Replace the formal register where legal certainty is required</li>
  <li>Resolve every title-specific issue in a transaction</li>
  <li>Assume that a missing result means a company has no property exposure at all</li>
</ul>
