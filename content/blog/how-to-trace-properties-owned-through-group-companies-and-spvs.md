<h2>Look for patterns, not just exact name matches</h2>
<p>SPV-heavy ownership structures often reveal themselves through repeated correspondence details, shared officers, or naming conventions rather than a single obvious proprietor name. The registry search should therefore be part of a broader tracing workflow that also includes Companies House and title-level review.</p>
