<h2>Why regional concentration matters</h2>
<p>Overseas ownership stories are rarely just national. Legal teams, journalists, and researchers often need to understand <strong>where</strong> ownership appears to cluster so they can decide which local markets, counterparties, or projects deserve closer review.</p>

<h3>What to look for in the data</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Regional concentrations around development corridors and core commercial districts</li>
  <li>Repeated proprietor-address patterns that suggest coordinated ownership structures</li>
  <li>Differences between portfolio-style holdings and isolated one-off titles</li>
</ul>
