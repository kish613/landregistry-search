<h2>Government search is essential, but not always sufficient</h2>
<p><a href="https://www.gov.uk/search-property-information-land-registry" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">GOV.UK property search</a> is the right place to go when you need a direct title document for a known property. A paid corporate-ownership search becomes useful when the hard part is not ordering the document, but <strong>figuring out which entity or titles you should investigate in the first place</strong>.</p>

<p>That is the commercial gap this site is trying to fill for legal and due-diligence teams.</p>
