<h2>Use both records in one pass</h2>
<p>Start at <a href="https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Companies House</a> to validate the entity. Then move immediately into corporate ownership search to see whether the company surfaces in CCOD. The combination is faster and more reliable than treating company filings and land ownership as separate investigations.</p>

<h3>Ten-minute checklist</h3>
<ol>
  <li>Confirm the entity number and filing status.</li>
  <li>Run the company number in the registry search.</li>
  <li>Review titles, proprietor names, and address patterns.</li>
  <li>Escalate only the material matches to full title document review.</li>
</ol>
