<h2>A useful angle for legal-tech and transparency coverage</h2>
<p>Concentration studies can turn raw ownership records into a story. By aggregating CCOD records by district, region, or proprietor pattern, teams can identify hotspots where corporate ownership clusters around development zones, logistics corridors, or investment-heavy markets.</p>

<p>These are the kinds of studies that can earn citations and backlinks because they reveal something broader than a single title search.</p>
