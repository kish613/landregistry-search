<h2>Use a repeatable process</h2>
<ol>
  <li>Validate the entity at Companies House.</li>
  <li>Screen the entity or address against CCOD data.</li>
  <li>Review any matching titles and proprietor signals.</li>
  <li>Order formal title material where the matter is transaction-critical.</li>
  <li>Record what was screened, what matched, and what still needs escalation.</li>
</ol>

<p>A checklist makes the search output defensible and easier to hand over across teams.</p>
