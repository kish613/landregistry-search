<h2>Choose the workflow that matches the question</h2>
<p>If you are asking who owns a known site, start with address. If you are screening a target entity, start with company number. If you are working through an individual with multiple corporate connections, director search can reveal the company set before property screening begins.</p>

<p>That sequencing matters because it reduces paid-search waste and gives legal teams a tighter set of titles to review.</p>
